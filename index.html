Who is Gordon Lightfoot?

Gordon Lightfoot was considered one of Canada's greatest singer/songwriters.

He composed many hits including The Canadian Railroad Trilogy and The Edmund Fitzgerald.

Gordon Lightfoot was noted for achieving international recognition writing songs about Canada.

On May 1st, 2023 Gordon Lightfoot passed away at the age of 84.

Rest in peace Gordon Lightfoot.

What Song Means to Me:

My family has always enjoyed Gordon Lightfoots music.

Of the many songs we listen to this one has always been my favorite.

Admittedly I didn't really understand the lyrics of Rainy Day People when I was a kid. But I always found the song calming.

Today I understand the lyrics and can appreciate the song a lot more than I used too.
